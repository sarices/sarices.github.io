flickr 200张了

You’ve run into one of [_the limits of a free account](http://www.flickr.com/help/limits/)_. Your free account will only display the most recent 200 photos you’ve uploaded. All of your photos beyond 200 will remain hidden from view until you either delete newer photos, or [**_upgrade to a Pro account**](http://www.flickr.com/upgrade/)_.

None of your photos have been deleted, and if you upgrade, they’ll all come back unharmed._
